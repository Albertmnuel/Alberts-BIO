About CBAS

The College of Basic and Applied Science (CBAS) at Mountain Top University is dedicated to advancing education and research in the fields of science, technology, and innovation. CBAS provides a strong foundation in scientific principles and practical applications across various departments including Computer Science, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.
